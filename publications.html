Publications

The SAWGraph project has produced peer-reviewed research and technical publications focused on ontologies, knowledge graphs, and their applications to environmental contamination monitoring and decision-making. Below is a selection of our key publications covering advances in semantic web technologies, geospatial analysis, and environmental data integration.

  4. CompTox Ontology: Leveraging Knowledge Graphs for PFAS Monitoring and Decision-Making Y Zhang, S Moavenzadeh et al.

About Our Research

Our publications demonstrate the application of semantic web technologies and ontology-driven approaches to solve real-world environmental challenges. We focus on areas including:

  • Ontology Design: Creating robust, reusable ontologies for environmental contamination and sampling, spatial features of interest, hydrology and chemical informatics
  • Knowledge Graphs: Leveraging knowledge graphs for integrated geospatial analysis and decision support
  • Contamination Monitoring: Supporting PFAS monitoring and other emerging contaminant tracking
  • Spatial Reasoning: Precomputed approaches for efficient spatial relationship analysis
